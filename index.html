Find Your Inner Strength

Inner strength is the ability to assess a situation, observe your own thoughts and emotions, and respond wisely and compassionately

Benefits

  • Improves strength, balance and flexibility.
  • Helps with stress relief.
  • Helps improve sleep.
  • Improves mental health and general well being.

Group Lessons

We offer two-week courses for individuals or small groups in a range of areas including stress reduction, improving your posture and so on.

One-to-one-lessons

We offer three-week one-to-one Lessons where you get complete attention, support, and adjustment. This is especially useful if you desire to deepen your practice, as the classes will be tailored specifically to your condition and needs.
